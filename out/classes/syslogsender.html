Class SyslogSender

SyslogSender sends messages to a syslog server.

It does not check the length of the message, which is limited do 1kB in legacy RFC3164 syslog implementations, and could be limited to 2kB in RFC5424 and/or RFC6587 implementations.

see

https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc3164#section-4.1

see

https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc5424#section-6.1

see

https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc6587#section-3.4.1
